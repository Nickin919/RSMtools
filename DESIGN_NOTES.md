# Design notes (WAIGO Figma / codebase alignment)

UI follows WAIGO design system for consistency:

- **Colors:** WAGO green `#00A651`, dark green `#007A3D`, blue `#0066A1`, gray `#58595B`. Primary buttons: `bg-wago-green`, hover `bg-wago-darkgreen`.
- **Components:** `.btn`, `.btn-primary`, `.btn-secondary`, `.card`, `.input`, `.container-custom`.
- **Font:** Inter, system-ui.
- **Layout:** `bg-gray-50`, `text-gray-900`, cards with `rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-gray-200`.

Reference: WAIGO Figma (file WAIGO) and WAIGO App `frontend/tailwind.config.js`, `frontend/src/index.css`.
